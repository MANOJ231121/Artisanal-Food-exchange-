Intro Video

With the increase in demand of community food organisations, it is important, now more than ever, to shape the way we view, grow and use food. That is why the online Food Exchange Platform was developed; so that as a community, we are able to become more sustainable.

We aim to reduce food waste and simplify access to fresh food, helping community food organisations to receive the supplies they need to support the communities they serve.

Who We Are

The Food Exchange Platform was envisioned to address how food is viewed and used. Connecting businesses, organisations, and individuals across local regions.

Our activities connect local growers and allotment holders with local food providers and food banks to build food security from the ground up, reducing shipping footprint and food miles.

Our Mission

Built specifically to improve and support the use of fresh food. Our primary aims are:

Reduce Food Waste

Reduce fresh food waste by minimising unsuitable food donations and redirecting surplus garden harvests.

Community Voice

Give local communities a voice in what food is provided through local community distribution centers.

Improve Consistency

Improve consistency and shelf life of fresh seasonal produce available in local supply centers.

Simplify Donations

Simplify the way in which fresh food is shared and improve the effective local use of donations.
